TeacherGen@I

TeacherGen@I is a teacher simulation integrated with generative AI that allows pre-service teachers to interact with students in a virtual classroom environment without burden. In this simulation, users can utilize their prepared teaching materials and speak verbally to students at their target level. Each virtual student agent with a unique persona is powered by generative AI and provides real-time responses reflecting learning objectives, class context, and their individual characteristics.

Key Features

Personalized Class Setup

Create your ideal classroom environment with customizable settings.

Teaching Materials Integration

Seamlessly integrate your teaching materials and lesson plans into the simulation.

Voice Interaction

Natural audio-based communication with virtual students for an immersive teaching experience.

GenAI Student Agents

Interact with AI-powered student personas that respond realistically to your teaching.

Activity Logs & Feedback

Receive detailed feedback and track your progress through comprehensive activity logs.

Mission-Based Teaching Simulation

Engage in real-world teaching scenarios where you must achieve specific goals, enhancing your problem-solving skills in a dynamic teaching environment.
